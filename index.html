Gabriel G. Carvalho
Ph.D. Student | Researcher

Bio: I am currently a Ph.D. student in computer science and a researcher at Projeto CIn-Motorola. My background is in mathematics (B.Sc. and M.Sc.), with a focus on differential geometry and topology, and I hold a Ph.D. degree in physics/relativistic astrophysics from the University of Rome "La Sapienza", under the supervision of Prof. Remo Ruffini and D. Bini. My current interests are computational photography, computer vision, machine learning, computational neuroscience, data science, differential geometry, topology, mathematical aspects of general relativity, and alternative theories of gravity.
